I consider that I do not have a personal style as an artist, I am constantly changing as a result of my learning process. I've been through several stages where my style varied from manga to portraits, but the central axis of my illustrations has always been women. The biggest change in my style happened along with my entry into the world of NFTs just 3 months ago, where I have since decided to dedicate myself more seriously to illustration.

This illustration is the third piece of my first NFT collection on OpenSea entitled Life Cycle of a Woman. After going through birth and childhood we arrive at youth, that stage in which we find ourselves in the prime of life. It is precisely at this stage where the beauty of the woman reaches its maximum splendor and is surrounded by a purely erotic essence.
